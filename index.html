Books

Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels written by J. K. Rowling.

The main character is Harry Potter who studies at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry with his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. The villain in these stories is Lord Voldemort who wants to oppress Muggles (non-magical people) and intend to be immortal. Fierce battles between Harry and Voldemort are the best parts. I'm sure the normal but magnificent lives at Hogwarts will bewitch you and take you inside the books!

Famous Quote from Hermione Granger -> "Books! And cleverness! There are more important things! - Friendship! And Bravery!"

My favorite characters -> Severus Snape, Luna Lovegood, and Hermione Granger

Music

Piano Concerto No. 3 in C major, Op. 26, is a piano concerto by Sergei Prokofiev. It was completed in 1921 using sketches first started in 1913. It is well known as a sophisticated passage that includes solo parts. The opening is so exciting and engaging! I highly recommend you listen to this masterpiece even if you are not interested in classical music.
